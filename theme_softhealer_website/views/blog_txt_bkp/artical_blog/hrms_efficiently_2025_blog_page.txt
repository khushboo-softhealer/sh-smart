                                    </h2>
                                    <p class="sh_text">

                                        Here are some of the most common challenges IT companies face when managing remote teams:

                                    <h3>
                                        1. Lack of Real-Time Attendance and Time Tracking
                                    </h3>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        Without physical check-ins, it's tough to monitor when employees start and end their workday. Manual tracking is error-prone and time-consuming.                                     <p class="sh_text">

                                        3. Difficulty in Monitoring Performance
                                    </h3>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        Tracking the progress and productivity of remote employees is a major concern. Managers need clear visibility into tasks, goals, and KPIs, which traditional systems rarely provide.
                                    </p>
                                    <h3>
                                        4. Disorganized Employee Records
                                    </h3>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        Managing employee data across spreadsheets and multiple tools is inefficient. IT firms need centralized employee data management systems to store contracts, resumes, performance records, and more in one place.
                                    </p>
                                    <h3>
                                        5. Low Employee Engagement &amp; Retention
                                    </h3>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        Remote workers may feel isolated or disconnected from the company culture. Without timely recognition, feedback, or wellness initiatives, retention rates can drop.
                                    </p>

                                        6. Complex HR Workflows
                                    </h3>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        From onboarding to exit interviews, handling the employee lifecycle management process remotely can become complicated. Many IT firms struggle to maintain consistency in their HR workflows.
