                                        WhatsApp Appointment Notifications
                                    </h3>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        Keeping patients informed is an important part of running a successful diagnostic lab. With the help of a pathology lab management system, labs can send appointment details and updates directly through WhatsApp. This makes communication fast, simple, and more reliable.
                                    </p>

Patients appreciate this because they don’t have to call the lab to check their status—they get updates instantly on their phones.
                                    </p>
