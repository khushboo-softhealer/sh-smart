                                    <h2 class="sh_heading_text">
                                        What is a Survey Management System?
                                    </h2>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        A survey management system is a digital platform that helps businesses create, manage, distribute, and analyze surveys from one place. Unlike simple form builders, these systems are designed to handle both basic and advanced survey needs with ease.
                                    </p>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        It allows users to build custom surveys using different types of questions and input fields—such as text boxes, checkboxes, file uploads, and even QR codes. You can also apply themes, set conditions, and organize sections to match your branding and survey goals.
                                    </p>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        Beyond survey creation, a good system also manages responses, tracks participation, and helps you understand results through built-in reports or data exports. It saves time, reduces errors, and ensures your data is always well-organized.
                                    </p>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        Whether you are collecting customer feedback, running employee engagement surveys, or launching product research, a survey management system gives you the tools to do it efficiently and professionally.
