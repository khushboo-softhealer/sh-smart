                                    <h2 class="sh_heading_text">
                                        What is a Pathology Lab?
                                    </h2>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        A pathology lab is a medical facility. Here, samples like
                                        blood, urine, and tissues are tested. These tests help
                                        diagnose and monitor diseases.
                                    </p>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        Pathology labs are important in healthcare. They give
                                        doctors information to choose treatments.
                                    </p>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        Labs can work alone or be part of hospitals and research
                                        centers. They handle simple blood tests and complex genetic
                                        studies.
                                    </p>
                                    <br />
                                    <h2 class="sh_heading_text">
                                        What is a Pathology Management System?
                                    </h2>
                                    <p class="sh_text"> A <a href="/pathology-lab-management"
                                            target="_blank"> Pathology Lab Management System </a> is
                                        a digital tool that handles all lab tasks. It manages
                                        patient registration, sample collection, test processing,
                                        result reporting, billing, and inventory. </p>
                                    <p class="sh_text">

                                        This software cuts down on manual work and reduces errors.
                                        It also helps labs work better. It makes data flow easily
                                        between departments, which improves teamwork and efficiency.

                                    </p>
