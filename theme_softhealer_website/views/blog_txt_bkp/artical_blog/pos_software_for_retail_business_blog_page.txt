                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        Today, modern POS software can do much more. It manages
                                        sales, tracks inventory, and keeps an eye on customer data.
                                        It also generates reports.

                                        Retail businesses use POS systems to provide good customer
                                        service. They help manage inventory and understand daily
                                        operations better.
