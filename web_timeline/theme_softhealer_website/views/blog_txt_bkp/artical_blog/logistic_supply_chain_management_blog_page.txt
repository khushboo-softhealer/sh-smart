                                    <h2 class="sh_heading_text">
                                        Understanding ERP Software in Logistics and Supply Chain
                                    </h2>
                                    <h3>
                                        What is ERP Software?
                                    </h3>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        ERP software is a central system. It links important
                                        business processes together. These include inventory
                                        management, order processing, transportation planning, and
                                        financial tracking.
                                    </p>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        It serves as one reliable source. This helps departments
                                        communicate more easily.
                                    </p>
                                    <h3>
                                        The Role of ERP in Logistics and Supply Chain Management
                                    </h3>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        In logistics, ERP software helps improve routing. It tracks
                                        shipments in real time and manages inventory efficiently.
                                        For supply chains, it helps with demand planning, working
                                        with suppliers, and seeing the whole process. This makes
                                        operations flexible and strong.
                                    </p>
