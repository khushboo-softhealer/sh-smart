                                     <h2 class="sh_heading_text">
                                    Core Modules of a Hotel Management System:
                                    </h2>
                                    
                                        <h3>Hotel Reservation System:</h3>
                                   
                                     <p class="sh_text">
                                       A key feature of hotel booking software is the reservation system. It manages bookings in real-time and tracks room availability across all channels. With a hotel booking system, you can manage online bookings easily. You can also handle direct reservations and third-party platforms all in one place. This reduces the risk of overbooking and ensures a smooth check-in process for guests.
                                    </p>

                                   
                                        <h3>Room Management:</h3>
                                    
                                     <p class="sh_text">
                                       With room management features, hotel staff can easily assign rooms. They can also check room status, like occupied, vacant, or under maintenance. This helps ensure that all housekeeping tasks are done on time. The ability to update room status in real-time leads to a smoother operation and a better guest experience.
                                    </p>

                                    
                                        <h3>Billing and Invoicing:</h3>
                                    
                                     <p class="sh_text">
                                       Hotel billing software is important for tracking guest expenses. This includes room charges, food and drink purchases, and other services. The software automatically generates invoices, reducing human error and ensuring that guests are charged appropriately.
                                    </p>

                                     
                                        <h3>Property Management System (PMS):</h3>
                                    
                                     <p class="sh_text">
                                       A property management system for hotels, often called a PMS, is essential for your hotel’s operations. It connects all of your property’s departments and operations, offering a centralized hub for managing bookings, guest information, financial data, and reporting.
                                    </p>

                                    
                                        <h3>POS (Point of Sale) Systems:</h3>
                                    
                                     <p class="sh_text">
                                       Connecting hotel software with POS systems makes it simple to manage transactions for hotel services. This includes restaurants, spas, and retail shops. This integration ensures that all charges are accurately captured and tied to guest folios.
                                    </p>
