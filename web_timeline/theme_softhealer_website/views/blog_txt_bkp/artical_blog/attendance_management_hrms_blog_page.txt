                                    <h2 class="sh_heading_text">
                                        What is Attendance Management?
                                    </h2>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        Attendance management is a key function in HR that tracks employee work hours, leaves, and overtime. It ensures accurate payroll calculations and compliance with company policies. Time and attendance software helps businesses track time automatically. It reduces manual errors and boosts worker productivity.
                                    </p>
                                    <p class="sh_text">
                                        With a good attendance management system, businesses can track attendance patterns. This helps reduce desertion and keep discipline in the workplace.
